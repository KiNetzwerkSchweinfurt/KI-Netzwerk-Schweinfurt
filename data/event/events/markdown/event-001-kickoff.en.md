## Founding Meeting of an AI Network in Schweinfurt

## Why an AI Network in Schweinfurt?

- **Strong industrial base:** Schweinfurt has experienced domain experts and many concrete application areas for AI.
- **Missing exchange space:** A regular, open format for practical AI exchange is still lacking.
- **Goal of the network:** Share experiences, lower barriers, and learn from one another.
- **Practical focus:** Real use cases, best practices, tools, and discussions.
- **Knowledge transfer:** Connecting industry, academia, and the developer community.

## Target Group

- People interested in AI and AI enthusiasts
- Industry and domain experts
- Software developers, engineers, and technical teams
- Students, researchers, and educators
- SMEs, start-ups, and decision-makers in the region

## Output / Outcome

- Strong networking with experts from industry, SMEs, and academia
- Know-how building through collaborative learning - learning is social
- Practical impulses for AI in development, engineering, and production
- Exchange on tools, standards, experiences, and real-world challenges
- Strengthening the visibility and future viability of Schweinfurt as a location
