# Welcome to the AI network

Our first meetup showed how strong the interest in practical AI is across the region.

## KI Netzwerk Schweinfurt - First Founding Initiative Meeting

**Location:** StudyFab Schweinfurt

## Goals and Expectations of the Participants

- Open exchange about artificial intelligence from technical, societal, and strategic perspectives
- Insights into concrete AI use in companies and the experiences gained from it
- Discussion about the impact of AI on society, education, and the world of work
- Exchange on data protection, intellectual property in AI usage, and local LLMs for protecting sensitive data
- Inspiration from other companies, for example on introduction, adoption, and change management
- Active participation in the network through knowledge sharing, practical contributions, and networking
- Transfer of AI knowledge to the wider public, including non-experts such as students and senior citizens
- Long-term perspective: building an AI lab for practical demonstrations and experiments
- Establishing a regional AI network in Schweinfurt
- Networking with local stakeholders and potential collaboration with THWS and students
- Using the network as a platform for practice, learning, and exchange

## Topics and Requested Focus Areas

- Practice-oriented formats in which experts present their experiences and use cases
- A practical use case for the next meeting around **ComfyUI** and vision models
  - Basic concept of ComfyUI
  - Distinction from other AI workflow frameworks such as n8n
- Additional topics:
  - local Small Language Models (SLMs) on PCs or mobile devices
  - relevant frameworks and tools such as Ollama, Google AI Edge Gallery, Gemma, Teuken, and Qwen
